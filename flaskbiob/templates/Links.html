Links to other cycling related resources

Blogs

Cycling Uphill

Tejvan Pettinger's site has some really good write ups of some of the best and steepest hills in the UK. A former UK hill climb champion, he knows a thing or two about... err... cycling uphill.

100 Climbs

Simon Warren's 100 climbs (and other related books) are a bit of a bible for UK riders seeking tough climbs. He's also got a blog with some decent content.

Epic Road Rides

An info packed site covering a massive variety of locations across Europe, including the UK. They've put a lot of effort into providing information to complete a whole trip as well as some sample routes.

Organisations

British Cycling

The governing body for cycling in the UK. The site is a good resource to find affiliated UK clubs and has great training resources as well

Cycling UK aka Cyclist's Touring Club

Cycling UK or CTC is a charity/ members organisation with lots of non-competitive touring focused riding groups, including the first club I was a member of, CTC Peterborough

Reviews and Magazines

Road.cc

A very good site reviewing a large array of cycling products

Podcasts and Youtube

Youtube can unfortunately attract a certain type that many find irritating but here's a few I enjoy

The Lanterne Rouge

Great Podcast and Youtube Channel. My favourite race analysis by far, really thorough, insightful and humerous content.

The Col Collective

The Col Collective videos are as inspiring as they are well made and presented. It's impossible to watch them without wanting to throw your bike in the back of the car and drive all the way to the alps.
